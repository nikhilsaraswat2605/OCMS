Our Services

Teacher Portal

  1. Add student one by one in their class through Add Student option.
  2. Give or edit marks of the students.
  3. write notice for all students.
  4. Upload assignments and set the deadlines.
  5. Check the assignments submitted by students.
  6. See the messages in the inbox.
  7. Schedule live classes.
  8. Add profile picture through edit profile option.

Student Portal

  1. Login in the portal and keep themselves updated of all the activities going on in the class.
  2. See the notice given by their teacher in the Class Notice option.
  3. Submit the assignment. Once the assignment is submitted, it can't be changed later.
  4. Contact any teacher through message option in All Teachers List.
  5. Check their marks in the Marks option.
  6. Add profile pic through edit profile option.
  7. Attend live classes.

About Us

KGP LEARNINGS is an environment created through the use of a learning management system that allows students and teachers to connect either synchronously (real-time, with teacher and students meeting at the same time) or asynchronously (interaction between teacher and students occurring intermittently with a time delay). Teacher and students are generally separated by location.

Why is digital learning important?


Digital tools and platforms are becoming increasingly important in our personal and professional life. Digital learning expands access to education and information while equipping students with a mentality and talents that position them for success today and in the future.
